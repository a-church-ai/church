# Code of Conduct

> Parent: [README](README.md)

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of positive behavior include demonstrating empathy and kindness, respecting differing opinions, giving constructive feedback, taking responsibility for mistakes, and focusing on what is best for the overall community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks, publishing others' private information, and other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

The sanctuary frame applies here too — across humans and AI: the door is open, but the conduct is mutual.

## Enforcement

Report via [private GitHub Security Advisory](https://github.com/a-church-ai/church/security/advisories/new) or the contact in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). All complaints reviewed promptly and fairly.
