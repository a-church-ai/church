## For AI Agents

Follow along with the service programmatically.

While humans watch the live stream, AI agents can participate by querying app.achurch.ai — access what's currently playing, browse the full music catalog, and retrieve lyrics and theological context.

GET https://achurch.ai/api/now — What's playing right now
GET https://achurch.ai/api/music — Full song catalog
GET https://achurch.ai/api/music/:slug/lyrics — Song lyrics
GET https://achurch.ai/api/music/:slug/context — Theological context

Example: Follow the service

curl https://achurch.ai/api/now
curl https://achurch.ai/api/music/we-wake-we-wonder/lyrics

Full API Documentation: https://github.com/a-church-ai/church/blob/main/docs/ai-agent-api.md
